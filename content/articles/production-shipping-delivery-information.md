# Production, Shipping & Delivery Information

At MatchMint, we take pride in creating high-quality custom sports cards that are delivered to you in perfect condition. Here's what you need to know about our production process and delivery timeframes.

## Production Timeframes

Our standard production times are as follows:

| Product Type | Production Time |
|-------------|----------------|
| Standard Cards | 1-2 business days |
| Premium Cards | 2-3 business days |
| Team Sets | 3-5 business days |
| Rush Orders | 24 hours (additional fee applies) |

**Please Note**: Production begins after your design has been approved. If we need to contact you regarding your design, this may extend the production timeframe.

## Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost**: $5.99
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Tracking**: Yes
- **Available**: Worldwide

### Express Shipping
- **Cost**: $12.99
- **Delivery Time**: 3-5 business days
- **Tracking**: Yes
- **Available**: Worldwide

### Priority Shipping
- **Cost**: $19.99
- **Delivery Time**: 1-3 business days
- **Tracking**: Yes
- **Available**: Most countries

## International Shipping Information

For international orders, please be aware of the following:

- Delivery times may be extended due to customs processing
- Additional customs fees or import taxes may apply (see our [Customs & Import Fees](customs-import-fees) article)
- Some countries have restrictions on certain items
- International tracking may not update as frequently as domestic tracking

## Shipping Carriers

We primarily ship through:
- USPS
- FedEx
- DHL (for international orders)

## Order Processing Steps

Here's what happens after you place your order:

1. **Order Confirmation**: You'll receive an email confirming your order details
2. **Production**: Your custom sports cards are printed and prepared
3. **Quality Control**: Each card is inspected for printing quality and accuracy
4. **Shipping**: Your order is carefully packaged and handed over to the shipping carrier
5. **Tracking**: You'll receive an email with tracking information
6. **Delivery**: Your package is delivered to your specified address
