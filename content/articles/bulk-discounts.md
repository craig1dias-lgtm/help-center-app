# Bulk Discounts

MatchMint offers substantial discounts for bulk orders, making it more affordable to purchase custom sports cards for teams, events, organizations, or large groups. This guide explains our volume pricing structure and how to place bulk orders.

## Bulk Discount Structure

Our standard bulk discount tiers are based on the quantity of cards ordered:

| Quantity | Discount | Example Savings* |
|----------|----------|------------------|
| 5-9 cards | 5% off | Save up to $11 |
| 10-19 cards | 10% off | Save up to $38 |
| 20-49 cards | 15% off | Save up to $142 |
| 50-99 cards | 20% off | Save up to $380 |
| 100-249 cards | 25% off | Save up to $937 |
| 250+ cards | 30% off | Save $1,125+ |

*Based on standard card pricing. Actual savings will vary based on card types and options selected.

## How Bulk Discounts Work

Our bulk discount system:

1. Automatically applies at checkout based on quantity
2. Applies to the card price (not shipping or add-ons)
3. Can be combined with seasonal promotions
4. Applies to identical or mixed card designs

## Bulk Order Process

For orders of 20+ cards, we recommend our streamlined bulk order process:

1. **Contact Us First**: Email bulk@matchmint.com with your requirements
2. **Complete Our Template**: We'll send you a spreadsheet to fill in all details
3. **Submit Images**: Provide all images in a single folder (via cloud storage)
4. **Review Quote**: We'll provide pricing including all applicable discounts
5. **Approve Design**: Review and approve digital proofs
6. **Production**: We'll create all cards according to your specifications

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I mix different card sizes in a bulk order?
Yes, but the discount tier is calculated separately for each size category.

### Do bulk discounts apply to premium finishes?
Yes, the discount applies to the base card price and premium finish options.

### Can I add more cards to my order later?
Yes, but additional cards will be processed as a new order. For best pricing, include all cards in the initial order.

### Is there a minimum order quantity for bulk discounts?
Our bulk discount structure begins at 5 cards, with increasing discount tiers for larger quantities.
