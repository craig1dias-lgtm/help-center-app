# What is Super Fast Track?

## Super Fast Track: Priority Production for Urgent Orders

Super Fast Track is our premium expedited production service designed for customers who need their custom sports cards produced as quickly as possible.

## How Super Fast Track Works

When you select Super Fast Track during checkout, your order receives:

1. **Priority status** in our production queue
2. **Same-day processing** (for orders placed before 1pm GMT/BST)
3. **Expedited production** with dedicated design staff
4. **Faster quality control** without compromising on standards

## Production Timeframes Comparison

| Service Level | Standard Production | With Super Fast Track |
|---------------|---------------------|------------------------|
| Design & Production | 3-5 business days | 1-2 business days |

## What's Included in Super Fast Track

### What Super Fast Track Includes:
- Expedited design and production process
- Priority handling by our production team
- Faster quality control and packaging
- Dedicated staff assignment to your order
- Priority customer service support

### What Super Fast Track Does NOT Include:
- Expedited shipping (shipping times depend on your chosen delivery method)
- Weekend or holiday production (business days only)
- Design proofs or additional revision cycles
- Rush customs processing for international orders

## When to Choose Super Fast Track

Super Fast Track is ideal for:

- **Gift emergencies**: When you need a last-minute gift
- **Special events**: When a celebration or presentation is approaching
- **Time-sensitive occasions**: Birthdays, graduations, or retirements
- **Replacement orders**: When you need a replacement quickly
- **Tournament deadlines**: When cards are needed for an upcoming event

## Cost of Super Fast Track

The Super Fast Track service is available for an additional fee, which varies based on your order size and complexity. The exact cost will be displayed during checkout when you select this option.

## Maximizing Delivery Speed

For the fastest possible delivery, we recommend combining Super Fast Track production with our express shipping options. This ensures both quick production and quick delivery.

### Recommended Combination:
1. Select Super Fast Track at checkout
2. Choose Priority or Express shipping
3. Place your order before 1pm GMT/BST for same-day processing
4. Ensure all design elements are complete and accurate to avoid delays

## Limitations and Availability

- Super Fast Track is subject to production capacity
- During extremely busy periods, Super Fast Track may be limited or unavailable
- Complex custom designs may require additional time even with Super Fast Track
- Some specialty products may not be eligible for Super Fast Track
