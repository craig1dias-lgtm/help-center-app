# Delivery Options & Shipping Information

At MatchMint, we offer several delivery options to ensure your custom sports cards reach you in perfect condition, no matter where you are in the world.

## Shipping Methods & Timeframes

Choose the shipping option that best meets your needs:

| Shipping Method | Cost | Delivery Time | Tracking | Best For |
|----------------|------|---------------|----------|----------|
| Standard Delivery | $5.99 | 5-7 business days | Yes | Non-urgent orders |
| Express Delivery | $12.99 | 2-3 business days | Yes, real-time | Time-sensitive orders |
| Next Day Delivery | $19.99 | Next business day* | Yes, real-time | Urgent orders |

*Order must be placed before 2pm local time for next-day delivery. Not available on weekends or holidays.

## Delivery Coverage

### Standard Delivery
- Available to over 180 countries worldwide
- Full tracking provided for most destinations
- Delivery confirmation included

### Express Delivery
- Available to most major countries
- Not available in some remote regions
- Full tracking with detailed updates

### Next Day Delivery
- Available in: US, UK, Canada, and select EU countries
- Limited availability in rural areas
- Guaranteed delivery timeframe (conditions apply)

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order by:

1. Clicking the tracking link in your dispatch email
2. Logging into your MatchMint account and viewing your order history
3. Contacting our customer support team with your order number

### Tracking Updates

Our tracking system provides the following status updates:
- Order Confirmed
- Processing
- Shipped
- In Transit
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered

For more detailed tracking information, see our [Tracking Your Delivery](tracking-delivery) guide.

## International Shipping Information

We ship to over 180 countries worldwide. Please note that international orders may be subject to:

- Import duties
- Taxes
- Customs processing fees
- Import restrictions

These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping fees. For more information, see our [Customs & Import Fees](customs-import-fees) guide.

## Shipping Insurance

All orders include basic shipping insurance that covers:
- Lost packages
- Severely damaged packages
- Theft (with police report)

For orders over $100, we recommend adding Premium Shipping Protection (+$3.99) which also covers:
- Minor damage
- Weather damage
- Extended coverage amount

## Delivery Exceptions & Delays

Please note that delivery times may be extended during:

- Holiday seasons (particularly November-December)
- Severe weather conditions
- Global shipping disruptions
- Customs processing (international orders)
- Rural or remote delivery locations

We'll always keep you updated if there are any delays with your order through email notifications.

## Delivery Address Requirements

For successful delivery, please ensure your address includes:
- Full name
- Street address with house/apartment number
- City, state/province, and postal/ZIP code
- Country
- Phone number for delivery notifications
- Special delivery instructions (if applicable)

## Need Help With Your Delivery?

If you have any questions about delivery or need to change your delivery address after placing an order:

- **Email**: support@matchmint.com
- **Subject**: "Delivery Question - Order #[Your Order Number]"
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours

For urgent delivery inquiries, please include "URGENT" in your email subject line.
