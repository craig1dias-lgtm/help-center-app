# VAT Registration Information

This article provides important information about MatchMint's Value Added Tax (VAT) registration status, how VAT is applied to your orders, and related tax considerations.

- **Registration Date**: January 1, 2022
- **Tax Authority**: HM Revenue & Customs (UK)
- **Company Registration Number**: 12345678

## How VAT Affects Your Order

The application of VAT depends on your location and status:

### UK Customers
- Standard UK VAT rate of 20% applies to all orders
- VAT is included in the displayed prices
- VAT breakdown is shown at checkout and on invoices

### EU Customers
- Local VAT rates apply based on your country
- VAT is calculated at checkout based on your delivery address
- VAT breakdown is shown at checkout and on invoices

### Non-EU Customers
- No UK or EU VAT is charged
- Local taxes, import duties, or sales tax may apply upon delivery
- These charges are the responsibility of the recipient

## Business Customers & VAT

### Business-to-Business (B2B) Orders

If you're ordering as a VAT-registered business:

1. **EU Businesses (outside UK)**:
   - Provide a valid EU VAT number during checkout
   - If validated, VAT will be reverse-charged (0% VAT)
   - Your business will account for VAT in your country

2. **UK Businesses**:
   - UK VAT will be charged regardless of VAT status
   - You can reclaim this VAT through your normal VAT return
   - VAT invoice will be provided for your records

3. **Non-EU Businesses**:
   - No UK or EU VAT will be charged
   - Local tax rules in your country will apply

## Requesting VAT Invoices

All orders automatically receive a standard receipt. For a VAT invoice:

1. **During Checkout**:
   - Select "I require a VAT invoice" option
   - Enter your business name and VAT number (if applicable)

2. **After Purchase**:
   - Email accounting@matchmint.com
   - Include your order number and VAT details
   - Subject line: "VAT Invoice Request - [Order Number]"

VAT invoices will be provided within 2 business days of request.

## VAT Exemptions

Certain customers may be exempt from VAT:

### Diplomatic Missions & International Organizations
- Official purchases may qualify for VAT exemption
- Appropriate documentation required
- Contact accounting@matchmint.com before ordering

### Charity Organizations
- UK registered charities may qualify for VAT relief on certain items
- Charity registration number required
- Restrictions apply based on product type

## VAT on Special Order Types

### Bulk Orders
- VAT is calculated on the discounted price after bulk discounts
- Business customers should provide VAT information before placing large orders

### Custom Projects
- VAT is applied to the total project cost
- Deposit payments include proportional VAT

## VAT Refunds

VAT refunds are available in these circumstances:

- Order cancellation
- Returned products
- Overcharged VAT (calculation errors)
- Qualifying business exemptions

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method and will include the VAT component.

## Changes to VAT Status

We will notify customers of any significant changes to:
- Our VAT registration status
- VAT rate changes
- Changes to cross-border VAT rules
