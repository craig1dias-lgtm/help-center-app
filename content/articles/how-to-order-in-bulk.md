# Bulk Ordering Guide

Ordering multiple custom sports cards for your team, club, event, or organization is easy with our bulk ordering process. Follow this guide to streamline your bulk order and save on your purchase.

## Benefits of Bulk Orders

- **Volume discounts**: Save more when ordering larger quantities
- **Consistent design**: Ensure all cards have a cohesive look
- **Simplified process**: One order for multiple recipients
- **Dedicated support**: Get personalized assistance for large orders
- **Priority production**: Bulk orders receive specialized handling

## How to Place a Bulk Order

### Option 1: Standard Bulk Order (5-20 Cards)

For smaller bulk orders, you can use our standard ordering process:

1. Visit our website and select the card type you want
2. Choose the quantity you need
3. For each card, provide the required customization details
4. Upload all necessary images
5. Complete checkout as normal

### Option 2: Large Bulk Order (20+ Cards)

For larger orders, we recommend our streamlined bulk order process:

1. **Contact us first**: Email bulk@matchmint.com with your requirements
2. **Complete our bulk order template**: We'll send you a spreadsheet to fill in all player details
3. **Send images**: Provide all player images in a single folder (via Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
4. **Review your quote**: We'll provide pricing including any applicable bulk discounts
5. **Approve and pay**: Once you're satisfied, we'll send a payment link
6. **Production begins**: We'll create all cards according to your specifications

## Image Requirements for Bulk Orders

To ensure smooth processing of your bulk order:

1. **Consistent image format**: Provide all images in the same format (preferably JPG or PNG)
2. **High resolution**: Minimum 1000px in height/width
3. **Clear file naming**: Name each image file to match the corresponding player
4. **Similar composition**: Try to maintain similar poses and backgrounds
5. **Organized delivery**: Provide images in a single ZIP file or shared folder
