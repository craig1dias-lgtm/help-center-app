# Damage Protection Plan

At MatchMint, we understand how important your custom cards are to you. That's why we offer a comprehensive Damage Protection Plan to ensure your cards remain in perfect condition from our facility to your hands.

## What is Damage Protection?

Our Damage Protection Plan is a service that safeguards your order against damage during shipping and handling. For a small additional fee, you can ensure that your cards are protected against various types of damage that might occur after they leave our facility.

## Coverage Details

### What's Covered

Our Damage Protection Plan covers:

- **Transit Damage**: Bent, creased, or crushed cards during shipping
- **Water Damage**: Moisture exposure during transit
- **Corner Damage**: Bent or damaged corners
- **Surface Scratches**: Scratches on the card surface
- **Color Fading**: Premature fading due to environmental factors
- **Edge Wear**: Damage to card edges during handling

### Coverage Duration

The Damage Protection Plan covers your cards for:

- **Standard Protection**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Extended Protection**: 90 days from delivery date (premium option)

## How to Add Protection to Your Order

Adding Damage Protection to your order is simple:

1. Add your desired cards to your cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Select "Add Damage Protection" in the options section
4. Choose your preferred protection level
5. Complete your purchase

## Making a Claim

If your cards arrive damaged despite the protection plan:

1. **Document the Damage**: Take clear photos of the damaged cards
2. **Contact Us**: Email support@matchmint.com within 7 days of delivery
3. **Include Details**: Provide your order number, photos, and description of damage
4. **Choose Resolution**: Request replacement or refund

## Recommended Storage for Cards

To maximize the effectiveness of your Damage Protection Plan:

- Store cards in protective sleeves (included with Premium Protection)
- Keep cards away from direct sunlight
- Store in a cool, dry environment
- Avoid excessive handling
- Use card holders or display cases for valuable cards

## Limitations and Exclusions

The Damage Protection Plan does not cover:

- Intentional damage
- Normal wear and tear beyond the coverage period
- Damage from improper storage or handling
- Lost or stolen cards
- Damage from natural disasters
- Cards modified after delivery (e.g., signed, altered)
