# What to Do When Your Order Arrives Damaged

We take great care in packaging our custom sports cards to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. However, we understand that shipping damages can occasionally occur. If your order has arrived damaged, we're here to help resolve the issue quickly.

## Step-by-Step Damage Resolution Process

### 1. Document the Damage

As soon as you notice damage to your order:

- Take clear photos of the damaged packaging
- Take clear photos of the damaged items
- Keep all packaging materials and damaged items
- Note any visible damage to the shipping box

These photos will help us assess the damage and process your claim more efficiently.

### 2. Contact Our Support Team

Contact us within **7 days** of receiving your damaged order:

- **Email**: support@matchmint.com
- **Subject Line**: "Damaged Order - [Your Order Number]"
- **Include in your email**:
  - Your order number
  - Description of the damage
  - Photos of the damaged items and packaging
  - Your preferred resolution (replacement or refund)

### 3. Our Resolution Process

Once we receive your damage report, our customer service team will:

1. Review your submitted information and photos (within 1-2 business days)
2. Confirm the damage is eligible for replacement/refund
3. Process your replacement order or refund
4. Provide you with tracking information for your replacement (if applicable)

## Types of Damage We Cover

We cover damage that occurs during shipping, including:

- Bent or creased cards
- Water damage
- Torn or ripped cards
- Scratched or scuffed surfaces
- Damaged corners or edges
- Printing errors not visible in digital proofs

## Replacement vs. Refund Options

In most cases, we offer:

- **Full replacement**: We'll reprint and ship your damaged items at no cost
- **Partial replacement**: If only some items were damaged
- **Refund**: In cases where a replacement isn't possible or preferred

## Timeframe for Resolution

| Resolution Step | Estimated Time |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Initial response | 24 hours |
| Review process | 1-2 business days |
| Replacement production | 1-3 business days |
| Shipping | Depends on location |

For urgent replacements, we offer expedited processing at no additional cost for damaged order replacements.

## Do You Need to Return Damaged Items?

In most cases, you will **not** need to return damaged items. However:

- We may request return of damaged items in certain situations
- If requested, we'll provide a prepaid return shipping label
- Returns must be sent back within 14 days of receiving the return label

## Prevention Tips for Future Orders

To minimize the risk of shipping damage:

- Consider premium shipping options for valuable orders
- Add shipping insurance during checkout for high-value orders
- Provide accurate delivery instructions for safe placement
- Request special packaging for delicate or high-value orders
