# Price & Size Guide

At MatchMint, we offer a variety of card sizes and finishes to meet your needs. This guide will help you understand our different options and pricing structure.

## Card Size Options

We offer four standard sizes for our custom sports cards:

| Size | Dimensions (inches) | Dimensions (mm) | Best For |
|------|---------------------|-----------------|----------|
| Standard | 2.5" x 3.5" | 63.5mm x 89mm | Trading card size, collections |
| Medium | 4" x 6" | 101.6mm x 152.4mm | Desk display, small frames |
| Large | 5" x 7" | 127mm x 177.8mm | Wall display, signed items |
| Extra Large | 8" x 10" | 203.2mm x 254mm | Featured displays, gifts |

## Card Thickness & Material

All our cards are printed on premium cardstock with these specifications:

- **Standard Cards**: 350gsm professional cardstock with UV coating
- **Premium Cards**: 400gsm cardstock with matte finish and spot UV highlights
- **Ultimate Cards**: 450gsm cardstock with textured finish and holographic elements

## Size Comparison Guide

Not sure which size to choose? Here's a helpful comparison:

- **Standard Size**: Similar to traditional trading cards and sports cards
- **Medium Size**: Approximately the size of a standard photograph or postcard
- **Large Size**: Similar to a standard greeting card when folded
- **Extra Large Size**: Similar to a standard sheet of paper
