# Affiliate Program & Payment Guide

## About Our Affiliate Program

The MatchMint Affiliate Program allows content creators, influencers, and website owners to earn commission by promoting our custom sports cards. As an affiliate, you'll receive a unique referral link to share with your audience, and you'll earn a percentage of sales generated through your link.

## Commission Structure

Our standard commission rates are:

| Sales Volume (Monthly) | Commission Rate |
|------------------------|-----------------|
| 1-10 orders | 10% |
| 11-25 orders | 12% |
| 26-50 orders | 15% |
| 51+ orders | Negotiable |

Special promotional periods may offer increased commission rates, which will be communicated to all active affiliates.

## Qualifying for Commission

For a sale to qualify for commission:

1. The customer must click through your unique affiliate link
2. The purchase must be completed within 30 days of clicking your link
3. The order must be successfully delivered and not returned/refunded
4. The customer must not have previously purchased through another affiliate link

## Payment Methods

We offer several payment methods for affiliate commissions:

- **PayPal** - Most popular option, available worldwide
- **Bank Transfer** - Available in select countries
- **Store Credit** - 10% bonus when choosing this option
- **Cryptocurrency** - Bitcoin or Ethereum (minimum payment threshold applies)

## Payment Schedule

Affiliate payments are processed on the following schedule:

- **Monthly Payments**: For affiliates earning more than £50/$50/€50 per month
- **Quarterly Payments**: For affiliates earning less than £50/$50/€50 per month

Payments are typically processed within the first 10 business days of the month following the earning period.

## Payment Thresholds

Minimum payment thresholds apply:

- **PayPal**: £20/$20/€20
- **Bank Transfer**: £50/$50/€50
- **Store Credit**: No minimum
- **Cryptocurrency**: £100/$100/€100

Earnings below these thresholds will roll over to the next payment period until the threshold is reached.

## Tracking Your Earnings

You can track your affiliate performance through our affiliate dashboard:

1. Log in to your affiliate account at affiliate.matchmint.com
2. View real-time statistics on clicks, conversions, and earnings
3. Access detailed reports on customer behavior and popular products
4. Generate custom tracking links for specific campaigns

### Dashboard Features

- **Performance Overview**: See your total earnings, clicks, and conversion rate
- **Sales Reports**: Detailed breakdown of each sale attributed to your links
- **Traffic Analysis**: See which content drives the most conversions
- **Payment History**: Complete record of all payments made to you
- **Marketing Materials**: Access banners, product images, and promotional text

## Tax Information

As an affiliate, you are responsible for reporting and paying taxes on your earnings according to your local tax laws. We may require tax documentation (such as a W-9 form for US affiliates) before processing payments.

For affiliates earning over certain thresholds, we may issue tax forms:
- US affiliates: 1099-MISC for earnings over $600/year
- UK affiliates: Payment statements for all earnings
- EU affiliates: Documentation compliant with local regulations

## Affiliate Program Best Practices

To maximize your earnings:

1. **Create authentic content**: Share your genuine experience with our products
2. **Target the right audience**: Focus on sports enthusiasts, collectors, and gift-givers
3. **Use high-quality images**: Showcase our products in the best light
4. **Track performance**: Use UTM parameters to identify your best-performing content
5. **Promote during peak seasons**: Holidays, major sporting events, and graduation season

## Common Payment Issues

### Delayed Payments
Payments may be delayed if:
- Your payment information is incomplete or incorrect
- You haven't submitted required tax documentation
- There are security concerns with your account

### Missing Commissions
If you believe you're missing commissions:
1. Check that the sale occurred within 30 days of the customer clicking your link
2. Verify that the order wasn't returned or refunded
3. Contact our affiliate support team with the order details

## Becoming an Affiliate
