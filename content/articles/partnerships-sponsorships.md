# Partnerships & Sponsorships

MatchMint offers various partnership and sponsorship opportunities for organizations, teams, content creators, and businesses. This guide outlines our collaboration programs and how to get involved.

## Types of Partnerships

We offer several types of collaboration opportunities:

### Team & Club Partnerships

Designed for sports teams at all levels:
- Youth sports organizations
- Amateur and semi-professional clubs
- School and university teams
- Professional sports organizations

### Content Creator Partnerships

For individuals with engaged audiences:
- Sports content creators
- Social media influencers
- Streamers and video creators
- Bloggers and journalists

### Business Partnerships

For complementary businesses:
- Sports equipment retailers
- Event management companies
- Photography studios
- Sports venues and facilities

### Charity & Community Sponsorships

Supporting causes and community initiatives:
- Sports-related charities
- Youth development programs
- Community events
- Fundraising initiatives

## Partnership Benefits

| Partnership Type | Benefits |
|-----------------|----------|
| **Team Partnerships** | Team card packages, bulk discounts, revenue sharing, custom team templates |
| **Content Creators** | Custom affiliate links, exclusive discounts, free samples, co-branded content |
| **Business Partners** | Cross-promotion, wholesale pricing, co-branded products, referral program |
| **Community Sponsorships** | Product donations, financial support, promotional materials, event presence |

## Team Partnership Program

Our most popular partnership program offers sports teams:

### Benefits for Teams
- **Discounted Team Cards**: Up to 40% off bulk orders
- **Custom Team Templates**: Exclusive card designs for your team
- **Fundraising Opportunities**: Use our cards for team fundraising
- **Digital Team Album**: Online showcase of your team's cards
- **Team Store**: Custom online store for your players and fans

### Eligibility Requirements
- Registered sports team or organization
- Minimum of 10 team members
- Commitment to annual card production
- Social media presence preferred but not required

## Content Creator Program

For influencers and content creators:

### Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Follower Requirement | Commission Rate | Additional Benefits |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Bronze | 5,000+ | 10% | Free sample cards |
| Silver | 20,000+ | 12% | Custom discount code |
| Gold | 50,000+ | 15% | Co-branded products |
| Platinum | 100,000+ | 20% | Custom partnership terms |

### Content Requirements
- Regular promotion of MatchMint products
- Authentic content featuring our cards
- Minimum monthly posting requirements
- Adherence to brand guidelines

## How to Apply

To apply for any of our partnership or sponsorship programs:

1. **Complete the Application Form**: Visit [matchmint.com/partnerships](https://www.matchmint.com/partnerships)
2. **Submit Required Materials**:
   - Organization/personal profile
   - Social media statistics (if applicable)
   - Partnership goals and objectives
   - Proposed collaboration ideas
3. **Initial Review**: 5-7 business days
4. **Consultation Call**: If application passes initial review
5. **Partnership Agreement**: Finalization of terms and conditions
